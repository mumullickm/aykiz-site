Delete Your Data

Aykiz is a single-user app. The only user is Miraz Uddin Mullick. There are no third-party accounts and no shared databases.

Local app data

All data lives on the device. To erase it: uninstall the app from the device, or use the app's Settings > Backups > Reset to clear the local store.

OAuth tokens (Google)

If Gmail or Calendar were ever connected, revoke the app's access at myaccount.google.com/permissions. Find "Aykiz" in the list and click Remove Access.

Conversations with Claude

Chat threads exist locally on the device. Deleting them from the app removes the local copy. Anthropic retains conversations under its own policy at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.
